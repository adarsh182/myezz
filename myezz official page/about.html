About MyEzz

MyEzz is Mumbai's first hyperlocal street food delivery platform that brings authentic street food directly to your doorstep in just 10 minutes.

Our Mission

To organize the unorganized street food sector by connecting authentic local vendors with hungry customers through technology, ensuring quality, speed, and accessibility.
